# Alex Lieberman

## Post 1

Topic:
Building AI-powered tools for collaboration on the web

Summary:
Alex describes how he built a tool that turns any website into a collaborative, “multiplayer” space similar to Google Docs. The idea came from frustration with current feedback workflows when reviewing documents, prototypes, or web pages. Instead of switching between tools and messages, users can comment, ask questions, and interact directly on any URL.

Key idea:
AI and collaboration tools can drastically improve feedback loops by making static content interactive and shared in real time.

Insight for B2B SaaS:
There is a growing opportunity in SaaS to improve collaboration workflows by embedding real-time interaction into existing digital assets (docs, websites, and internal tools).

## Post 2

Topic:
The rise of “Marketing Engineers” and AI-driven marketing workflows

Summary:
Alex introduces the concept of a “Marketing Engineer,” a marketer who not only uses AI tools but also builds systems, workflows, and processes powered by AI. He explains that modern marketing is evolving into a hybrid role that combines creativity, technical thinking, and systems design.

He shares his experience building an AI-driven “Content Machine” that turns raw ideas into finished social content, highlighting how marketers must understand both strategy and how to structure AI workflows effectively.

Key idea:
The future of marketing belongs to people who can think in systems, design workflows, and combine creativity with AI-driven automation.

Insight for B2B SaaS:
Marketing teams will increasingly need hybrid skills (strategy + systems thinking + AI workflows), creating demand for tools that help marketers build scalable content and growth systems.
